LS Len

Established: April 30, 2025

Introduction to LS Len

LS Len specializes in providing handcrafted yarn-based products, accessories, keychains, and decorations that are safe and of high quality.

"LS Len" was created with the desire to become a symbol of RESPONSIBLE BUSINESS by combining structured, profitable business operations with the delivery of long-term, meaningful values for customers, employees, partners, and the community.

Our Story

#1. With Employees

LS Len aims to build an open organization and a dynamic environment with its own unique culture. Here, you will be able to fully unleash your potential and receive fair salaries and bonuses. In addition to the salary, employees of LS Len also receive annual ESOPs (Employee Stock Ownership Plan) as a reward for their valuable contributions.

#2. With Customers

LS Len strives to deliver better value to customers through a direct sales model via the e-commerce platform. Instead of marking up products 4 to 6 times the cost as traditionally done, LS Len commercializes at a markup of 1.8 to 2.5 times, while still ensuring a reasonable profit. Additionally, LS Len focuses on offering a better shopping experience for customers by placing a strong emphasis on customer service.
